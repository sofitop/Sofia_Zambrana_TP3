Awareness Poster Poster

/poster design

Our world as we know it's constantly changing for the worse, and it's our fault. One of the problems I wanted to talk about is how the habitat of certain penguins in the Patagonia is being compromised by our own trash. It has been found that these penguins are living among plastic waste, including the common “reusable” plastic bags from supermarkets. How much longer do we have to wait until everyone sees how big of a deal this really is?
